00169 /*----------------------------------------------------------------------------
00170                         Note: choice of library version
00171 ------------------------------------------------------------------------------
00172 QTouch libraries are supplied built with a range of charge times.
00173 Charge time is the duration in which charge is transferred from the AVR onto
00174 the measurement capacitor. Shorter charge times result in faster measurements,
00175 but may be too fast to completely transfer charge onto the measurement
00176 capacitor.
00177 
00178 The range of charge times lets the user choose a suitable version for the clock
00179 rate their chip is running at.
00180 
00181 To make capacitive measurements, a charge time on the order of 0.25us to 2.5us
00182 is typically required. The following table shows the charge times corresponding
00183 to a range of clock speeds.
00184 
00185 Clock speed (MHz) Cycle time (us) Suitable charge times
00186 ----------------- --------------- ---------------------
00187       1              1            1~2 cycles (1us to 2us)
00188       2              0.5          1~5 cycles (0.5us to 2.5us)
00189       4              0.25         1~10 cycles (0.25us to 2.5us)
00190       8              0.125        2~10 cycles (0.25us to 1.25us)
00191       10             0.1          3~25 cycles (0.3us to 2.5us)
00192       16             0.0625       4~25 cycles (0.25us to 1.5625s)
00193       20             0.05         5~50 cycles (0.25us to 2.5us)
00194 
00195 ----------------------------------------------------------------------------*/
